Meet Calvin Van Auken

About Me

Hello there! Im Calvin (Cj) Van Auken. I am 19 years old and am in pursuit of becoming a developer! Ever since I was 13, I had an interest in tech. One day, my Grandma (Meme) brought up a career path I did not know much about. She brought up working for Google and all of these big tech names. I was 13. I didn't even know what or how I could start. Around this time, I was looking for a high school and came across a fantastic new school where you can study a trait to use once you graduate. There was a coding and game dev class that my family and I were very excited about! Sadly, all of the spots were filled. I would have to choose a different trait or attend a regular public school. I then chose Graphic Design. Let's say I wasn't the happiest with it. The coding class was right next door to the Graphic Design room. After my freshman year, I decided to split paths with the school, leaving me at a typical public school. Fast forward to when I graduated... I was stuck and unsure of what I should do with my career. I never enjoyed school. A four-year university was out of the picture, so what now? One night, I was sitting in bed when coding came to mind. For the next month, I dedicated my days to learning code and understanding it. And towards the end of a YouTube video: I got an ad for a coding boot camp. I was sucked in from there. Now, being 19 and having a career path I love and enjoy doing, I can't wait for what the future can present me with.
